The Resources to Create Greatness



Boyertown holds education at its highest priority to prepare students for their post high school years. Although, learning by experience far outweighs the knowledge gained from sitting and listening to a lecture especially in the Technology and Engineering fields. For this reason, Boyertown prizes itself on providing students new and advanced technology from screen printing, 3D printers, robotics, technical drawing programs, and architure design programs at their disposal. By allowing the students to familiarize themselves with equipment in their career field, it provides a head start when continuing to a technical college or career. This experience can not be matched to other students that have simply learned from a book or ancient programs. Below are images of each room and a convenient list of the equipment in each room.
